About Scouting4U

Scouting4U was established in 2010 with the goal of providing all stakeholders involved in the basketball community with access to a reliable, up-to-date and comprehensive statistical and video database of players and games.

With a comprehensive understanding of the dynamics of the market, our goal has been to create an online marketplace where in-depth information about players is available, where coaches can aggregate information and where agents are provided with the tools that are essential for them to showcase candidate-players.

Scouting4U is one of the leading services in the world servicing the realm of basketball scouting and delivering advanced statistical analysis. Our unique and highly competent services combine the largest database of online basketball games and the most sophisticated statistical software that serves a multitude of customers. In the near future we plan to broaden our geographical footprint and introduce our services to additional sports.

Scouting4U was founded by Daniel Gutt and Mikey Gorka.

Mr. Gutt is a basketball coach and scout who have worked with tier-1 European basketball teams for many years. Over the years, he developed a fast and completely automated tool that creates and analyzes statistical reports in order to establish a much more serious and more reliable standard for professional scouting. Over the past few years Mr. Gutt has been engaged with the Israeli Basketball Academy educating promising young basketball players.

Mr. Gorka graduated from Spring Hill College with a degree in international business. He played professional basketball in the Israeli Division1 league and started his coaching career in the Israeli Division1 league in 2012. He has served as Head Coach of Maccabi Haifa and Beni Herzlia.
